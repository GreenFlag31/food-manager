This app lets you manage your food products, keeping track of the expiry date. Never waste food again !

Avoid wasting food is good for the planet and your wallet.

Useful information about this topic can be found at the FAO : Tips for reducing food waste

Just create an account | Log in and insert the name and the expiry date of a product. No email validation is required.

As I'm not a web designer, I found inspiration in the following design : Chris Meyer on dribbble
